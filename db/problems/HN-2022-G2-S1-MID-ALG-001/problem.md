## Q
$\sqrt[3]{3}\times\sqrt[3]{9}$을 간단히 하면?

## Choices
① $3$
② $9$
③ $15$
④ $21$
⑤ $27$

## Answer
①

## Solution
\[
\sqrt[3]{3}\times\sqrt[3]{9}
=\sqrt[3]{27}
=3
\]
